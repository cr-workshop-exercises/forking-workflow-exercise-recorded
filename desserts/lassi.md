## Ingredients
- ice cubes
- 1 ¾ cups plain yogurt
- 1 ½ cups ice water
- 6 cubes ice, crushed
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- 1 pinch salt

# Instructions
- Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes
- Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy.
- Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve
